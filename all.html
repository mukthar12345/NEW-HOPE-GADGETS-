    Our Humble Beginnings

    New Hope Gadgets started in 2015 as a small kiosk in the bustling Computer Village of Lagos. Our founder, Chinedu Okoro, noticed that many Nigerians struggled to access quality technology at affordable prices while also having limited options for selling or repairing their devices.

    What began as a one-person operation has now grown into a trusted technology hub serving thousands of customers across Lagos. Despite our growth, we've stayed true to our original mission: making technology accessible, affordable, and sustainable for everyone.

    Today, we're proud to be one of the most reputable gadget stores in Computer Village, known for our fair prices, quality products, and exceptional customer service.

    Our Mission & Vision

    At New Hope Gadgets, our mission is to democratize technology ownership in Nigeria by providing access to quality, affordable gadgets and exceptional service to all.

    We envision a future where every Nigerian can easily access the technology they need to learn, work, connect, and thrive in the digital age—without breaking the bank or worrying about reliability.

    We believe that technology should empower people, not burden them. That's why we're committed to transparency, quality, and customer education in everything we do.

    Our Core Values
