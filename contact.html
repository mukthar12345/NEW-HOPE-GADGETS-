Frequently Asked Questions

How long do repairs usually take?

Most repairs are completed within 24-48 hours. Complex issues may take longer, but we'll provide an estimated timeframe when we diagnose your device.

Do you offer warranty on your products?

Yes, all our products come with a 30-day warranty. Refurbished devices come with a 90-day warranty for your peace of mind.

Can I get a price estimate before visiting?

Absolutely! You can call us or fill out the contact form with details about your device, and we'll provide an estimated price range.

Do you buy broken devices?

Yes, we buy devices in all conditions. The price will depend on the device model, age, and condition.
